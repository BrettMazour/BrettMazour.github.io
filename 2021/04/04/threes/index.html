The 3-point shot has become a staple of NBA basketball. There’s nothing like watching 3-point marksmen such as Steph Curry, Kyle Korver, and Damian Lillard drill contested threes in the opponent’s face with perfect shooting form. These days, many NBA team’s offenses are centered around the 3-point shot, but this hasn’t always been the case.

Steph Curry Steph Curry definitely helped create this migration to the behind the 3-point line. We have never seen someone shoot the basketball as good as him. He’s the greatest shooter to ever play, and I don’t think it’s close. I believe his style of play helped revolutionize the entire game of basketball. Don’t believe he’s the greatest shooter ever?
